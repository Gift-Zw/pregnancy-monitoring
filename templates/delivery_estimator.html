Delivery Day Estimation

The Delivery Date Estimation page helps expectant mothers estimate their due date based on their last menstrual period (LMP) and menstrual cycle length. By entering the first day of your last menstrual period and your cycle length, the tool calculates your estimated due date by adding approximately 280 days (40 weeks) from the LMP, adjusting for cycle length variations. This method provides a reliable estimate for planning prenatal care and preparing for your baby's arrival.
